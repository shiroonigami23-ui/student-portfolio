## üéÆ How to Use

1. **Sign In:** Use your Google account to access the dashboard.
2. **Create New:** Click **"+ Create New Portfolio"** or use **"‚ú® Generate with AI"** to start.
3. **Build:** Follow the numbered tabs (1-6) to input your data.
   - *Tip:* Use the "Improve Writing" button if you get stuck on descriptions!
4. **Customize:** In the final tab, select your preferred template and color scheme.
5. **Save & Share:** Save your portfolio and get a shareable link (or export your data).
